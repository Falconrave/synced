Find Friends

1. Choose People -> Profiles -> Find People to start connecting up with your friends.

2. Type in a friend's real name, email address or Whirled (display) name and click Search to find their Profile.

3. Click on their name to view their profile page.

4. Click Send Mail to send them a mail in Whirled or click Invite to be Your Friend to add them to your Friends list.

5. It's easy to meet new people in Whirled too, especially with Groups. Choose People -> Groups to see the wide range of friendly Whirled communities.
