Change Your Avatar

Let's go shopping!

1. Choose Stuff -> Catalog -> Avatars to see a few of the avatars you can get.

                           2. Browse through the various avatars. Click one you like and buy it!

3. To wear it, choose Stuff -> My Stuff -> My Avatars and click the the little happy face next to your new avatar.

4. Click the close box to close your inventory and see your spendid new self!
