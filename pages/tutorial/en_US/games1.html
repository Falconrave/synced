Play Games

Whirled has a growing collection of fun games and playing them earns you Flow!

1. Choose Catalog -> Games to check out the popular games.

2. Click on a game to read more about it and click Play! to open the game's lobby.

3. Click Join on a table waiting for players to join that game, or click Create Table to start your own game.

4. By playing games, you'll earn Flow which you can use to buy cool Avatars and fancy Furniture and all sorts of other great stuff from the Catalog.
