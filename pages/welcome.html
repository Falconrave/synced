Welcome to the First Synced!

Synced is a social online world for chat and multi-player games, powered by player-created content. Virtue Development' With Synced we are trying to do something different -- in Synced the players create the characters, rooms and (in due course) the games. We hope that this will be a lot of fun for everyone!

To get started, you should head into the Synced. You will need to login with your email address and the password you were sent. Please then click "Me" -> "Account" and configure your Permaname which will allow you to log into the Forums and the Wiki. You can access the Forums and Wiki from the menu navigation in the Synced once you've logged in.

We will be active on the forums and in the world so feel free to post questions and comments there or say hello if you see us in Synced.

Thanks again for coming and playing around with our very incomplete new project. We look forward to seeing and playing with the cool stuff you make.
